Bachelor of Theology/Christian Ministry Degree Program

Veenstra Theological Seminary, in conjunction with the University of Jos, to which it is affiliated, offers 4 and 3 year full time courses leading to a Bachelor of Theology Degree. This degree is also offered through a part time program over 4.5 years to 6 years. This B.Th program is designed for students who have not yet undertaken any theological studies. However, admission to this program is also available to holders of Diploma in Theology or its equivalent from any recognized institution. These Diploma holders shall be admitted to the 200 level. The Bachelor of Theology Degree at VTS is presented in four tracts as areas of specialty. The four areas are: Biblical Studies, Historical and Theological Studies, Practical Theology, and Christian Education.

B.Th Course Requirement

a. The credit load required for the Bachelor of Theology Degree is 130 credit units as the minimum. These must be taken from the core and the electives in line with the areas of specialty.
b. A student may register for a maximum of 144 credit units.
c. Any student admitted to the 200 level will be required t complete not less than 87 credits at the third year (400 level).
It is also required that the B.Th student must pass Basic Hebrew and Basic Greek. The student may wish to pursue the study of Biblical Languages to the Further and Exegesis levels.
